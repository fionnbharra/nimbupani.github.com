When gays marry

Dan Savage writes a very insightful article on how the sanctity of marriage is violated when gay men marry women. There is no law prevent them from being married and being gay at the same time (as the recent scandal showed). Why not lift the ban on gay marriage and prevent such mockery of marriages (if that is the aim of the “ban on gay marriage”)?
