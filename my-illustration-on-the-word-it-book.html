My Illustration on The Word It Book

I finally got my copy of The Word It Book which is a compilation of illustrations done for the “Word-It” topic every week at “The Speak Up” site. “Word It” is a section where people illustrate a word. For e.g. my illustration was for the word “star”. And I am so happy they picked my illustration to put in the book!

Here is my illustration on page 169, with a short “interview” with me :)

I am delighted! This is my first “book publication” YAY!
