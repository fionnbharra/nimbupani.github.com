Missing in Action

…yet again! I am hurrying up to finish my first oil painting before I leave.

Need to fix my site too in time for CSS Reboot - given that I scratched and shift deleted a design I had been working on in a moment of designer’s frustration. But I know a bulb will light some eerie corner of my brain by the end of this weekend :)

I also visited the Galerie Belvedere for an exhibition of Indonesian artists which were surprisingly too few a number. All of them seem quite Gauguinish.

Oh did I tell you guys I am going away? Taman Negara beckons! Canopywalk here I come!
