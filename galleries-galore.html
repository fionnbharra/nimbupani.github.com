Galleries Galore

I submitted my site, on a whim, to the cream of the CSS design showcase sites about two months ago. About a week ago I noticed through Google Alerts that my website has been linked from so many of these galleries including: CSS Princess (yay!), Web Creme, Screenfluent, CSS Mania, Guspim’s Design Solutions Patterns sets, Best Web Gallery (managed by the awesome ndesign-studio).

Update: Design Shack and Creating a Blog Design also linked to the site. Thank you!

Update 2: CSSVault (another YAY!), CSSArtillery (the site is so beautiful it can be kissed!), Webdesign Inspiration

A big thank you to all of you for featuring my website! Meanwhile, you can also follow how my website is battling other websites on CommandShift3.
